Monica: Why don't I just take off my clothes and have a nightmare.

Woman: It's okay, it's okay, I'm here, I'm here. I'm so sorry I'm late, okay, here I am. Hi, who's the new tense girl?

[Scene: Central Perk, scene continued from earlier.  They guys are sitting there like the Three Monkeys.]

And that thought scared me more than all the other crap I was afraid to do.
